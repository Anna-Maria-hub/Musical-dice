Welcome to "Musical Game of Dice"!

This project is the result of a solo endeavor undertaken within the context of a University of Vienna course. With a passion for music, history, and technology, I embarked on this journey to explore a unique perspective on music composition. Driven by the goal of merging academic learning with practical application, this project aims to breathe life into Johann Philipp Kirnberger's musical dice game in a digital format.
As a sole contributor, I have dived deep into historical research, web development, and design to bring you an immersive experience. This project is a testament to the intersection of my passion for music and technology, offering an opportunity to explore one of Johann Philipp Kirnberger's books through a digital lens. The website's source code is available on GitHub, allowing you to see how it was built and explore its features.

I extend my gratitude to the University of Vienna for providing me with the opportunity to pursue this project. This endeavor has not only enriched my understanding of music history but has also allowed me to showcase my skills in web development.

Thank you for exploring this project with me!
Your interest and curiosity are greatly appreciated!
